Hello. My name is Tavinder
and I love data.

Are you swimming in data and do not know what to do with it?

​You have come to the right place!

We are in an era of collecting data. There is so much that can be learned from data, but often we do not know what to ask of our data, analyze or interpret what it is telling us to make informed decisions.

We realize that it is hard to come across good statisticians and psychometricians outside of the University setting. Data for research and non-research purposes is collected everywhere and we recognize the importance of data management as key so that less time is spent trying to organize data than running analyses. Setting up your data management from the start is essential to save time and money later.

Data Capture

We are skilled in designing surveys and data capture strategies that will allow you to capture the information you want from users. We also can assess the reliability and validity of ﻿measurement tools under development.﻿​

Analysis

We provide technical and conceptual support in both simple (e.g., descriptive statistics) and complex statistical analyses (e.g., multivariate analyses such as bootstrapping, mediation/moderation models, and structural equation modelling).﻿​

Output

We assist in the statistical interpretation of findings and knowledge translation for manuscripts, presentations, and technical manuals.!
